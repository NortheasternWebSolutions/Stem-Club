About Us

Our club brings students together to solve problems and learn new skills.

What we do: We work on engineering challenges, coding projects, and science experiments in a collaborative environment.

Who can join: Everyone is welcome! You don't need prior experience, just an interest in STEM.

Our Projects

  • Coding: Learning Python and web development.
  • Engineering: Bridge building and structural design challenges.
